# Adeptus Mechanicus

The Adeptus Mechanicus is a faction within the Warhammer 40,000 universe, also known as the **Cult Mechanicus** or simply **the Mechanicus**.

## Overview

The Adeptus Mechanicus is the powerful, quasi-religious institution that holds a monopoly on technology throughout the Imperium of Man. They worship the **Omnissiah** (often considered an aspect of the Emperor) and pursue the acquisition of all knowledge and technology in the universe.

### Core Beliefs

- **The Machine God (Omnissiah)** - The supreme deity of technology
- **The Motive Force** - The divine spark that animates all machines
- **The Flesh is Weak** - Biological bodies are inferior to mechanical augmentation

## Key Units

### Skitarii

The Skitarii are the cybernetic soldiers of the Adeptus Mechanicus, also known as the **Tech-Guard** or **Skitarii Legions**.

#### Skitarii Vanguard

- Heavily augmented infantry
- Equipped with rad-weapons that emit dangerous radiation
- Often have bionic replacements for limbs and organs
- Wear protective armor with rebreather units

#### Skitarii Rangers

- Long-range specialists
- Equipped with galvanic rifles and transuranic arquebuses
- Often deployed as scouts and skirmishers

### Tech-Priests

- **Tech-Priest Dominus** - Commanders and battlefield engineers
- **Tech-Priest Enginseer** - Specializes in repairing vehicles and machinery
- **Tech-Priest Manipulus** - Boosts nearby units with galvanic fields

### Vehicles & Constructs

- **Onager Dunecrawler** - Walking tanks with powerful weaponry
- **Skorpius Disintegrator** - Anti-grav armored transport
- **Kastelan Robots** - Ancient, heavily armed battle automata
- **Serberys Raiders** - Fast cavalry units mounted on cybernetic steeds

## Playstyle

The Adeptus Mechanicus army is characterized by:

- **High Tech, Low Survivability** - Powerful weapons but fragile infantry
- **Doctrina Imperatives** - Tactical protocols that change unit behaviors
- **Canticles of the Omnissiah** - Buffs that activate like hymns
- **Vehicle Synergy** - Many abilities enhance or rely on machines

### Strengths

- Excellent shooting phase with high-strength weaponry
- Strong anti-vehicle capabilities
- Flexible army composition with many unit options
- Powerful character support abilities

### Weaknesses

- Low Toughness and Wounds on most infantry
- Complex army rules with many interacting abilities
- Expensive in points for what you get
- Struggles in melee combat

## Painting Scheme Ideas

## Lore

The Adeptus Mechanicus predates the Imperium itself, originating on Mars during the Age of Strife. They maintain ancient technologies that even they barely understand, treating technology with religious reverence.

The Mechanicus operates **Forge Worlds** across the galaxy - entire planets dedicated to industrial production and technological research. Each Forge World has its own traditions, specialties, and sometimes heretical beliefs.
